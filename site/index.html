About diff prez

With President Trump coming into office, many are up in arms about the policy changes that could and will occur. With diff prez, you can search key issues like Climate, Military and the Economy and see what kind of stances our current and most recent leader have taken.

Using the search bar above, search a topic that concerns you, and see what kinds of differences the two president's have in regards to the subject. We use official news, policies and other information from whitehouse.gov for searching and display them right up next to eachother for easy comparison.

This project was made for the BoilerMake IV Hackathon, a 36-hour coding marathon where over 500 hackers from across the country come together to create awesome stuff.
